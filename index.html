📘 Leçon

Canada is in Northern America, and its capital is Ottawa.
There are no States in Canada but Territories (Yukon) and Provinces (Ontario).
Inuits were the first inhabitants of Canada: they lived in the north (Nunavut).
Canada is surrounded by three oceans:
  • North → Arctic Ocean
  • West → Pacific Ocean
  • East → Atlantic Ocean
It has a border with Alaska in the northwest, and with the USA in the south.
Canada is the second largest country in the world after Russia.
